Understanding Risk Management

\

\ Risk management is a structured approach to identifying, assessing,\ and mitigating risks that may affect an organization\'92s objectives.\ This website provides foundational knowledge and practical insights\ into risk management practices.\

\ \

What is Risk Management?

\

\ Risk management is the process of identifying potential events that\ could negatively impact objectives, analyzing their likelihood and\ impact, and implementing controls to reduce exposure.\

\ \

Why Risk Management Matters

\

\ Effective risk management supports better decision-making,\ strengthens governance, protects organizational value, and improves\ resilience in uncertain environments.\
