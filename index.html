What is Risk Management?

Risk management is a structured process used to identify, assess, and mitigate risks that could affect the achievement of objectives.

Why Risk Management Matters

Better Decisions

Risk management makes uncertainty visible and manageable.

Stronger Governance

It supports oversight, accountability, and compliance.

Resilience

Organizations respond faster to disruption and change.
