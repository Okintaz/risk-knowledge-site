Risk Management Implementation Around the World

Risk management is implemented globally across industries such as finance, infrastructure, energy, healthcare, and technology. While approaches vary, the core principles remain consistent.

Project & Infrastructure

Large infrastructure projects use risk management to address cost overruns, schedule delays, safety risks, and regulatory compliance.

Financial & Investment Institutions

Banks and investment firms apply risk management to credit, market, liquidity, and operational risks to protect capital and maintain stability.

Public Sector & Government

Governments adopt enterprise risk management frameworks to improve policy decisions, service delivery, and crisis preparedness.

Across regions, effective implementation depends on leadership commitment, risk culture, and continuous monitoring.
