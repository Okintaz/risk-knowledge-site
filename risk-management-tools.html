Risk Management Tools

Risk management tools help organizations translate abstract uncertainty into structured, actionable insights. These tools support identification, assessment, monitoring, and decision-making across different risk types.

1. Risk Register

A risk register is the core repository of identified risks. It documents key information including causes, impacts, ownership, and mitigation actions.

3. Key Risk Indicators (KRI)

KRIs are measurable metrics used to provide early warning signals of increasing risk exposure.

Risk Area KRI Threshold Current Value Status Action Triggered
Financial Risk Portfolio value decline (%) > 15% 12% Normal Monitor
Operational Risk Late risk reports > 3 divisions 4 divisions Warning Escalation to management
Compliance Risk Unresolved audit findings > 5 issues 6 issues Critical Immediate remediation
